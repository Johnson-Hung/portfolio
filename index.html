SAVE - 羊 (August, 2019)

"SAVE" is a free and creative puzzle game. In this game, you are the white lamb trying to save all the black lambs and escape from the wolf. 

It's my first mobile game, and I spent around one month to finish this project. During the process, there was one thing that I remembered and enjoyed the most, that is, learning A* and  implement it to my AI (wolf), because I had heard of the term "A*" in game design course before, but I didn't understand it at all. Thanks to this project, I finally learned about what is A* and how to use that.
